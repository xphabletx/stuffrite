# Privacy Policy for Stuffrite

**Last Updated**: December 5, 2025

## Introduction
Stuffrite ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for authentication)
- Display name (optional, user-provided)

### Financial Data
- Envelope names and amounts
- Transaction records
- Budget groups
- Scheduled payment information

### Usage Data
- App preferences (theme, font, currency)
- Workspace membership (if joined)
- Device information (for crash reporting)

## How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the app functionality
- Store your budget data securely in Firebase Firestore
- Enable workspace sharing features
- Improve app performance

## Data Storage and Security
- All data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud)
- We use Firebase Authentication for secure login
- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- We do NOT sell your data to third parties

## Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)
You have the right to:
- **Access your data**: All data is visible in the app
- **Export your data**: Use "Export My Data" in Settings
- **Delete your data**: Use "Delete Account" in Settings
- **Opt-out**: Delete your account
